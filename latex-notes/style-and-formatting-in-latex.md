Style And Formatting
====================

## Font Style

|Style     |Command         |
|----------|----------------|
|Bold      |\textbf{text}   |
|Italics   |\textit{text}   |
|Underline |\underline{text}|

## Change Paragraph

To change paragraph `\par`.

## Place Text In Center

To put text in center use

```
    \begin{center}
    	your text here
    \end{center}
```
